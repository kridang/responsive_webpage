Da Nang has a tropical monsoon climate, characterized by warm temperatures year-round. The city experiences a dry season from February to August with sunny skies and moderate humidity, and a wet season from September to January, when heavy rainfall and occasional typhoons occur. Average temperatures range from 69.8°F in winter to 87.8°F in summer. The hottest period occurs in August, when high humidity can make the heat feel even more intense.
